Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13 June 2026

Octane Studio ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you share with us through this website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website to request a quote or inquire regarding our services, we may collect the following information:

  • First and/or Last Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Type of Service Requested
  • Budget information
  • Any additional message or information you include

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you regarding your inquiry or quote request
  • Better understand your service needs
  • Improve our services and customer support

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties.

3. Email and Communication

By submitting any form of contact or inquiry, you consent to being contacted by us regarding your inquiry. We will only use your information for direct communication related to your request.

4. Data Retention

We retain the information you provide only as long as necessary to fulfill your request and for basic business records. You may request we delete your information at any time by contacting us.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information but cannot guarantee complete security. Information submitted through the form is sent via standard email protocols and may not be encrypted.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
